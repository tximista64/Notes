### **SLA for Patching According to CVE**

**What is SLA?**

A service-level agreement (SLA) is a contract between a service 
provider and its customers that documents what services the provider 
will furnish and defines the service standards the provider is obligated
 to meet.

*source: techtarget.com*

Have you set up a maximum update time for your 
workstations/servers/softwares? If yes, do you take into account 
criteria such as the CVSS (Common Vulnerability Scoring System) score, 
the fact that the server to be patched is on the Internet-facing or not,
 that the flaw is known to be exploited (0-day)?
